# George Salib 🌌

Hi, I'm Geo, an insatiable learner and developer focused on the intersection of **computational science and software engineering** with some cybersecurity and hardware engineering in the mix. I've also led large-scale hackathons and technical community initiatives.

---

### 🚀 Focus & Expertise

- **Astrophysics & ML:** Building n-body simulation frameworks and training Random Forest models to classify astronomical data.

- **Cybersecurity:** Mentoring students in cybersecurity and working with the usual toolset suite (Wireshark, Nmap, hydra, and Burp Suite, etc...).

- **Core Tech:** Proficient in Python, with competency in C++ and Bash. Experienced with Git, Raspberry Pi, and hardware design tools like EasyEDA.

- **Community Leadership:** Organizing major hackathons like Scrapyard and Counterspell Giza and managing the STEM Egypt [Hack Club](https://hackclub.com) branch.

### 🛠️ Technical Toolkit

- **Languages:** Python (Proficient), C++ (Competent), Bash, MySQL, LaTeX.
- **Tech:** Linux (Ubuntu, Mint, RPiOS, Kali), Git, VirtualBox, Blender.
- **Hardware:** Raspberry Pi, Arduino, EasyEDA.

### 🌍 Impact & Leadership

- **Hackathon Leadership:** Lead Organizer for [**Scrapyard Giza**](https://stemeghackclub.org/Hackathons/scrapyard) and [**Counterspell Giza**](https://stemeghackclub.org/Hackathons/counterspell) (total 250+ attendees).
- **Community Growth:** Raised **$3,500+** in funding and supported over 1,000 students through technical mentoring & leadership.
- **Mentorship:** Served as **Vice President** & **Cybersecurity Mentor** of the [STEM Egypt Hack Club](https://stemegypt.hackclub.com).
